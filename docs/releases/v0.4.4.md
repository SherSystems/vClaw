## Self-healing when things heal themselves.

> TBD — landing alongside `feature/healing-recovery-and-wiring`.
> Expected scope: when an incident self-resolves (the underlying
> condition clears before RHODES executes a plan), the healing
> engine recognizes the recovery, cancels the pending plan, and
> closes the incident with a "recovered without action" record
> instead of forcing operator approval on a plan that no longer
> needs to run.

## Probes wired end-to-end.

> TBD — landing alongside `feature/healing-recovery-and-wiring`.

> auto-route through gather → classify → plan → approve → execute
> without programmatic kickoff. This is the wiring referenced in the
> v0.4.3 changelog note ("queued for a follow-up so the
